About

A bit about
me.

I was born and raised in Zambia, moved to the US at 14, and have been in Bloomington, IL since. I studied accounting at Taylor University and graduated in 2023. Since then I've been working at Lifesong for Orphans, a nonprofit that supports orphan care around the world. The work involves fund accounting and financial reporting across programs in several countries.


I'm married to my wife, Goodness. Outside of work and family, I started Social Brew, make music, help people with their taxes during tax season, and do occasional consulting work. A lot going on — but it all feels connected.

Social Brew

Social Brew is an app for finding and rating independent coffee shops. Every shop on the platform is independently owned — no chains, ever. Users rate visits using a Mug Fill Score, and shops get discovered through real activity, not paid placement. I started it because I wanted something like this to exist.
